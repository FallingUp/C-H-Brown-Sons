  • History

C H Brown & Son was established as a traditional greengrocer in 1935. Cyril Brown bought a newly built shop situated on a busy parade serving the new Glebe Farm council estate. Son Ken subsequently joined him and together they built up a chain of six greengrocers around Birmingham: Coventry Road, Brays Road, Alum Rock, Maryland Avenue and Stechford Lane in addition to the original Glebe Farm premises. There have been numerous changes over the years and currently we run two shops.

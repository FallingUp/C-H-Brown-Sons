16 Glebe Farm Road, Stechford has become a convenience store offering a wide range of groceries as well as services like newspapers and magazines, Lottery, mobile and energy top-ups. “Serving the community since 1935” is our proud motto, and we are ably assisted by loyal staff members, some of whom have been with us since 1997. Blake joined us at the age of 16 and now manages all aspects of the shop. He and the team are happy to help customers old and new.

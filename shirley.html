  • History

In 2015 we bought another traditional greengrocer's shop situated in Shirley, Solihull which was added to the Tom Brown's brand. The shop became available after the untimely death of local businessman Joe Richards. Joe had become a good friend to Tom and had been particularly encouraging as he gained experience in the trade. Joe was extremely influential and widely respected and will be long remembered. The staff team that he nurtured under the management of Maxine now keep the tradition alive through their continued work with C H Brown.
